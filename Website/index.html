Law Overview

Republic Act 7877

Also known as Anti-Sexual Haraddment Act of 1995. This law prohobits sexual harassment in places like educational institutions, workplace and training environments. This aims to protect people from unwanted sexual advances and other forms of sexual misconduct.

Why is law important in protecting women and children?

    The law will be their last resort of help when the situation cannot be helped furthermore the law will guarantee them the upmost safety and security they can get.

National Program (Framework for Women)

    This is a structured approach that addresses various aspects of a womans life, empowerment and development. Examples are:
  • Health and well-being
  • Human rights
  • Social and Economic empowerment, etc.

Civil Society (UKP-network for women in politics)

    Also known as "Ugnayan ng mga Kababaihan na Pulitika". The goal is to support candidates, both male and female who advocatefor women's rights and empowerment. The agenda includes pressing issues like upholding international humanitarian law, penalizing sexual harassment, ect.
